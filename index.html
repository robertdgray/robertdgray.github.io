Robert D. Gray

I am a Reader in Pure Mathematics, working in the School of Mathematics of the University of East Anglia.

I am an algebraist with interests in a wide range of topics including: combinatorial and geometric group and semigroup theory, homological finiteness properties, algebraic combinatorics, groups acting on graphs and other relational structures, and homogeneous structures in the model-theoretic sense.
