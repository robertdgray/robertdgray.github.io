Robert D. Gray

I am a Professor of Mathematics and an EPSRC Research Fellow working in the School of Mathematics of the University of East Anglia.

I am an algebraist with interests in a broad range of topics including: combinatorial and geometric group and semigroup theory, algorithmic problems in algebra, decidability, homological finiteness properties, algebraic combinatorics, and groups and monoids acting on graphs, relational structures, and topological spaces.
